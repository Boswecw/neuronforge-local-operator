# Lore Safe Test 003

Amicae stood beneath the ash-lantern and watched the ridge in silence. There were nights when the dark seemed only dark, and there were nights when it looked back. This was the latter sort, and she liked it not.

The horse below the rise stamped once, then were still. No wind moved through the thorn trees, yet their shadows shifted upon the frost as if some old procession passed between the world and whatever lay just beyond it.

"If they come before first light, let them come," she said. "I have no prayers left for such men."

Rawn, who had heard that tone before and knew its cost, said nothing for a time. At last he answered, "Then keep your strength. Vengeance is a blade that turns in the hand if held overlong."
