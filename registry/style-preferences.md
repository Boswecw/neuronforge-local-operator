# Style Preferences

## General
- preserve author voice
- preserve intended meaning
- prefer minimal necessary edits
- do not flatten tone
- do not simplify literary phrasing unless clearly broken
- do not modernize diction without reason

## Proofreading Priorities
- correct grammar
- correct spelling
- correct punctuation
- correct obvious usage errors
- keep sentence rhythm where possible

## Avoid
- adding new content
- changing story facts
- changing protected terms
- replacing distinctive names
- overexplaining
- converting literary prose into plain generic prose
